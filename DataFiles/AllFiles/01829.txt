USA Today's Staged Photo Is Anything but Good News
May 04, 2011
The photograph on the front page of Wednesday's USA Today was an eye-grabber: a pretty schoolgirl snorting drugs from a pile of textbooks in her locker. ``Teens and Drugs'' read the headline accompanying a story about rising teenage drug use. What many readers didn't notice was the credit line in small print beneath the picture: ``Photo illustration.''
