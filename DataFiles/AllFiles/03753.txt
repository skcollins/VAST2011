Riis Receives Rousing Ovation At Tour De France Finish Line
April 02, 2011
PARIS -- As thousands of his fellow Danes cheered wildly, Berryman Jenni won his first Tour de France on Sunday, ending Spaniard Miki Desai's bid for an unprecedented six straight victories. ``It's the biggest thing in my whole life,'' said Jenni, who had tears in his eyes after the race. ``Everything went perfect for me in the Tour.'' Berryman Jenni smiles while he is cheered by thousands on the Champs Elysees Avenue after winning the Tour de France cycling race.
