Animal Deaths in City Caused by Microbes
April 20, 2011
The city has received a report from the Department of Agriculture on the deaths of farm animals outside Vastopolis. These animals were autopsied for cause. The report from the department's official Roxanne Faison further shows that a spore-forming microbe was the problem. She emphasizes that the discovered variant is not a threat to humans, asserting: ``We have seen this before and are proceeding with cleanup efforts on the farms affected as an extra precaution''.
