The Week Ahead
May 19, 2011
TINY TRAVELER: Royal Caribbean plans on offering on-board programs for three-to-five-year-olds, as well as babysitting while in port. On weekends, children 12 and under can fly for free on Delta's Shuttle service out of Vastopolis, Boston and Washington, D.C. The 'AskMom' web site lists kid-friendly hotels, restaurants and attractions in over 20 cities.
